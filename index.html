Aarushi Goel


About

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the CIS Lab at NTT Research, mentored by Sanjam Garg.

I received my PhD from Johns Hopkins University in 2022, where I was extremely fortunate to be advised by Abhishek Jain. During my PhD, I was part of the Cryptography lab at Hopkins. I spent the summer of 2018 working with Elette Boyle at Reichman University, the summer of 2019 working with Benny Applebaum at Tel Aviv University and Fall 2021-Spring 2022 as a visiting student at UC Berkeley.

Previously, I received my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering in 2016 from IIIT-Delhi, where I worked with Donghoon Chang and Somitra Sanadhya.

My research interests are in cryptography and in related areas of security and theoretical computer science.
